If you are new to this area, it is a good idea and much safer and more fun to team up with others and not fly alone. For this you can join one or more of the THPK club Signal groups:

  • THPK flyboks: here we coordinate airspace and announce opening and closing of "boxes"
  • THPK tur: planning tours
  • THPK sosial: social stuff
  • THPK skitprat: random talk
